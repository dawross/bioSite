About Chloe

Chloe Ross is my 11-year-old daughter that lives with me 50% of the time. She is the world to me and really the only real family I have any more. Chloe enjoys playing video games (of course), drawing, reading, playing with our dog, YouTube, and lots of other usual kid stuff. She says she wants to be some sort of artist when she grows up, so I do what I can to make sure she has what she needs to keep pursuing that. Chloe does very well in school, even though she is very clear that she hates going. The current circumstances we’re all in with the pandemic is no problem for her since she does not have a problem with not going to school. I have yet to tell her that she has to start online this coming Monday!

We have a corgi named Gatsby, that is her absolute best friend. He is a great dog and is very protective of her, even though she tortures him with hugs and affection. She also has a cat named Sylvester, a snake named Noodle, and 2 leopard geckos named Quibble and Echo. She has lots of friends she enjoys hanging out with, so she does miss doing that since we can’t right now. We enjoy playing video games together. Some of her favorite video games are Undertale, Deltarune, Roblox, and Ark Survival.

There are plenty of pictures on the site to look at and lots of her art as well. I hope you enjoy looking at everything.
